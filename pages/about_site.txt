        [ ■ ] ABOUT SITE / PURPOSE
    </div>
    
    <p style="margin: 10px 0; line-height: 1.5;">
        Welcome to <span class="c-cmd">CODING HISTORY</span>--an interactive educational retro-themed terminal website created as a project for <span class="c-cmd">Readings in Philippine History</span>.
    </p>
    
    <p style="margin: 10px 0; line-height: 1.5;">
        This website was created to explore how technology and education can work together to make historical learning more engaging, interactive, and accessible. Instead of presenting Philippine History as a collection of dates and names to memorize, <span class="c-cmd">CODING HISTORY</span> allows users to explore historical topics through a terminal-inspired interface.
    </p>
    
    <p style="margin: 10px 0; line-height: 1.5;">
        The project was developed in response to the growing need for meaningful and engaging educational resources. The gap in Philippine historical education has made it increasingly important for students to have access to materials that encourage historical understanding, critical thinking, and informed engagement with the past.
    </p>
    
    <p style="margin: 10px 0; line-height: 1.5;">
        Through its interactive format, <span class="c-cmd">CODING HISTORY</span> aims to make learning feel less like passively reading a textbook and more like actively exploring a system. Each module serves as an entry point into different periods and aspects of Philippine history.
    </p>

</div>
